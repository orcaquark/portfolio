// about me

Hi! I'm a Computer Engineering student at UCSD. I believe modern devices have gotten too unrepairable and anti-consumer, and I want to fix that. Technology should respect the people using them, so it should be something that you can open, fix, and actually own. Additionally, I want to make sustainable technology that is designed to last and benefit others.

That said, a lot of what I do is motivated by the fact that building cool things is fun and challenging. Most of my time right now goes into Kindred, where I work on user analytics and feature development, as well as this site, which I view as a constant work in progress.

Outside of tech, I'm an astrophotographer and painter. I'm always looking forward to my next side quest, and most of the time you can find me on a walk, reading my next book, or playing my favorite games.
